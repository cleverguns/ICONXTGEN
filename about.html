Company Profile

iCONXTGEN Network and Data Solution is a company in the field of Security Systems (CCTV), and Data Infrastructure. The value we can provide extends well beyond safety issues of information privacy, fast and reliable network connectivity based on a high demand information technology. The increasing need of security, monitoring and controlling remotely homes, wide or small businesses, infrastructure and facilities are aspects of growing concern for private people, companies and institutions, as well as all types of communities (such as Barangay's, Subdivisions/HOA's and many more) Our goal is to provide a smart and cost-effecti ve solution by offering scalable security and communication solutions which would efficiently and effectively support the protection and preservation of properties, that allows to identify and detect intruders, raise alarms and communicate them to local or remote operators in real time.

Our ultimate goal is to pursue cooperation and achieve results in the best interest of both parties and attain a fast-growing relationship with our cli ents. Build confidence, safety and trust that is mutually beneficial for both. We also offer a complete line of products and customs solutions for wired and wireless networking systems and components, including routers, antennas, communication cabinet, power supplies, servers and others. We have a full spectrum of network personnel s and technical support teams who will cater services to our clients in the ever-changing market along with the fast-paced life having a higher demand of information privacy through technology. With a humble beginning, we are focused to achieve the best service we can provide our clients and ourselves. And build a trustworthy and better foundation to the best of our capacity. "In God We Trust" that the doors of opportunities will be open for iCON XTGEN Network and Data Solution to be of service to your needs.

Vision

We, at iCONXTGEN Network and Data Solution, envision a safer flow of communication services by providing the best security system and data safety through effective and efficient means.
